The MET Fifth Ave

Pretty much free entry if you live in New York and have proof of address! The MET is everyone's must-go. Even though I've been, I've really not. I've only visited one small section, I think. The section I want to visit the most is probably the Costume Institute, and the Arms and Armor? Please ask me out and go life drawing here with me! Side note, the gift shop is delightful too.

manhattan loved

Whitney Museum

Free entry if you present your Pratt student ID! Might be one of my personal favourite museums. It's somewhat small compared to some of the others on this list, but the exhibitions are always well curated and it's never too busy. I love the view outside the museum, and walking along the exterior staircase. So many happy memories made here!
